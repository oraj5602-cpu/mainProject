🏠 Address Correction Application

An Address Correction Application is used to request correction of an incorrect address, spelling mistake, house number, locality name, ward information, or other address-related details in municipal records and certificates.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Concerned Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Address Correction

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby request correction of the address recorded in the municipal records/certificate. The details are provided below:

  • Applicant Name: ______________________
  • Application/Certificate Number: ______________________
  • Current Recorded Address: ______________________
  • Correct Address: ______________________
  • Reason for Correction: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________

I have attached the necessary supporting documents for verification. Kindly update the records and make the required correction at the earliest.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for Address Correction

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation portal and select the "Address Correction" service.

Step 2: Fill the Correction Form

Enter the application or certificate details and provide the correct address information.

Step 3: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Original Certificate/Document Copy
  • Supporting Documents for Correction
  • Any Additional Documents Required by the Authority

Step 4: Pay Applicable Fee

Pay the prescribed correction fee, if applicable.

Step 5: Submit the Application

Review all details carefully and submit the correction request.

Step 6: Verification Process

The concerned department will verify the submitted documents and correction request.

Step 7: Record Update

Upon approval, the corrected address will be updated in the records and a revised document may be issued if applicable.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Ensure the new address matches the submitted proof documents.
  • Upload clear and readable copies of all documents.
  • Double-check address details before submission.
  • Keep the acknowledgement number safe for tracking.
