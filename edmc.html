🏛️ How to Use the EDMC (MCD) Online Portal

The East Delhi Municipal Corporation (EDMC) is now a part of the Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD). Citizens can access various municipal services online without visiting the office. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the official MCD online portal using your web browser.

Step 2: Select the Required Service

  • Birth Certificate
  • Death Certificate
  • Property Tax
  • Trade License
  • Building Plan Approval
  • Mutation Services
  • Water & Sewer Services
  • Citizen Complaints

Step 3: Login or Register

Create an account using your mobile number and email ID if you are a new user. Existing users can simply log in.

Step 4: Fill the Application Form

Enter all required details carefully and upload supporting documents wherever necessary.

Step 5: Submit the Application

Review your information and submit the application online. Save the application or acknowledgement number for future reference.

Step 6: Track Status

Use the application number on the portal to check the status of your request anytime. Many services such as certificates, property tax, and licenses can be tracked online. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Important Tips

  • Keep scanned copies of required documents ready.
  • Double-check all entered information before submitting.
  • Keep your registered mobile number active for OTP verification.
  • Save or print the acknowledgement receipt after submission.
