📢 Grievance / Public Complaint Application

A Grievance Application is used to submit complaints, suggestions, requests, or concerns regarding municipal services, public facilities, civic amenities, sanitation, roads, water supply, street lighting, parks, drainage, and other public issues.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Grievance Redressal Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Submission of Public Grievance

Respected Sir/Madam,

I would like to bring the following grievance to your notice and request appropriate action. The details are provided below:

  • Name of Applicant: ______________________
  • Address: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________
  • Category of Grievance: ______________________
  • Location of Issue: ______________________
  • Description of Complaint: ______________________
  • Date Issue Noticed: ______________________

I request the concerned department to investigate the matter and take appropriate corrective action at the earliest.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Submit a Grievance

Step 1: Visit the Municipal Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation grievance portal and select the "Public Grievance" service.

Step 2: Register/Login

Create an account or log in using your existing credentials.

Step 3: Fill the Grievance Form

Enter your contact details, grievance category, location, and a detailed description of the issue.

Step 4: Upload Supporting Documents

  • Photographs of the Issue (if applicable)
  • Relevant Documents
  • Any Additional Evidence Supporting the Complaint

Step 5: Submit the Application

Verify the information and submit the grievance online.

Step 6: Receive Reference Number

A grievance reference number will be generated for status tracking.

Step 7: Review & Resolution

The concerned department will review the grievance and take appropriate action based on the nature of the complaint.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Provide complete and accurate information.
  • Upload supporting evidence whenever available.
  • Clearly describe the issue and its location.
  • Keep the grievance reference number safe for tracking.
