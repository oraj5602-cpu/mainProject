Income Certificate Guide

How to Apply for an Income Certificate

  1. Visit your State Government's online citizen service portal or your nearest Tehsil/CSC office.
  2. Log in or create a new account.
  3. Select "Income Certificate" from the list of available services.
  4. Fill in the application form with:
    • Applicant's Name
    • Father's/Mother's Name
    • Date of Birth
    • Address
    • Occupation
    • Annual Family Income
    • Mobile Number
  5. Upload the required documents such as:
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Address Proof
    • Identity Proof
    • Income Proof or Salary Slip (if applicable)
    • Passport-size Photograph (if required)
  6. Review all entered details carefully.
  7. Pay the application fee if applicable.
  8. Click the Submit button.
  9. Save the Application Number or Acknowledgement Receipt.
  10. Track the application status and download the certificate after approval.
Note: The required documents and processing time may vary depending on your state or union territory. Always provide accurate information to avoid delays.
