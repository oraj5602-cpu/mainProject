🚧 Encroachment Complaint Application

An Encroachment Complaint Application is used to report unauthorized occupation or obstruction of public land, roads, footpaths, parks, drains, or other municipal properties. The Municipal Corporation may investigate the complaint and take action as per applicable rules.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Commissioner,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Complaint Regarding Encroachment on Public Property

Respected Sir/Madam,

I wish to bring to your notice an encroachment on public property at the location mentioned below. The details of the complaint are as follows:

  • Name of Complainant: ______________________
  • Address: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Location of Encroachment: ______________________
  • Nature of Encroachment: ______________________
  • Date Noticed: ______________________

The encroachment is causing inconvenience to the public and may affect the proper use of municipal property. I request the concerned department to investigate the matter and take appropriate action.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Complainant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to File an Encroachment Complaint

Step 1: Visit the Municipal Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation complaint portal and select "Encroachment Complaint".

Step 2: Fill the Complaint Form

Provide details of the encroachment, including its location and the nature of the issue.

Step 3: Upload Supporting Evidence

  • Photographs of the Encroachment
  • Location Details
  • Any Supporting Documents (if available)

Step 4: Submit the Complaint

Review the details and submit the complaint through the portal.

Step 5: Complaint Registration

A complaint or reference number will be generated for future tracking.

Step 6: Investigation & Action

The concerned municipal department may inspect the site and take action in accordance with municipal regulations.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Provide accurate location details for faster action.
  • Upload clear photographs whenever possible.
  • Avoid submitting duplicate complaints for the same issue.
  • Keep the complaint number safe for status tracking.
