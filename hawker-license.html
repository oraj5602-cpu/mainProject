🛒 Hawker / Street Vendor License Application

A Hawker or Street Vendor License is required for individuals who wish to conduct vending activities in designated public areas. The license helps regulate street vending activities and ensures compliance with municipal rules and public safety requirements.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Licensing Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Hawker / Street Vendor License

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby apply for the grant of a Hawker / Street Vendor License. The particulars of the applicant are provided below:

  • Name of Applicant: ______________________
  • Father's/Husband's Name: ______________________
  • Residential Address: ______________________
  • Type of Goods/Services Sold: ______________________
  • Proposed Vending Location: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________

I undertake to follow all applicable rules, regulations, and conditions prescribed by the Municipal Corporation regarding street vending. Kindly consider my application and issue the license.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for a Hawker / Street Vendor License

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation licensing portal and select the "Hawker / Street Vendor License" service.

Step 2: Register/Login

Create an account or log in using your existing credentials.

Step 3: Fill the Application Form

Enter your personal details, vending information, and proposed vending location.

Step 4: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Recent Photograph of Vending Cart/Stall (if applicable)
  • Any Additional Documents Required by the Authority

Step 5: Pay the Prescribed Fee

Complete the payment of the application or licensing fee through the available payment options.

Step 6: Verification Process

The concerned department may verify the submitted information and vending location details.

Step 7: License Issuance

Upon approval, the Hawker / Street Vendor License will be issued and may be downloaded from the portal or collected from the office.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Only vend in authorized or designated areas.
  • Keep the vending area clean and hygienic.
  • Carry the license while conducting business.
  • Renew the license before its expiry date.
