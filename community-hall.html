🏛️ Community Hall Booking Application

A Community Hall Booking Application is submitted to reserve a municipal community hall for events such as marriages, social gatherings, cultural programs, meetings, educational activities, and other approved functions.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Community Hall Booking Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Community Hall Booking

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby request the booking of a community hall for the event detailed below. The required particulars are as follows:

  • Name of Applicant: ______________________
  • Residential Address: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________
  • Name of Hall (if known): ______________________
  • Purpose of Booking: ______________________
  • Date of Event: ______________________
  • Time Duration: ______________________
  • Expected Number of Guests: ______________________

I agree to comply with all rules, regulations, and conditions related to the use of the community hall. Kindly approve my booking request.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Book a Community Hall

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation portal and navigate to the "Community Hall Booking" service.

Step 2: Check Availability

Search for the desired community hall and verify availability for your preferred date and time.

Step 3: Fill the Application Form

Enter applicant details, event information, and booking requirements.

Step 4: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Any Additional Documents Required by the Authority

Step 5: Pay Booking Charges

Pay the prescribed booking fee and security deposit, if applicable.

Step 6: Verification & Approval

The booking request will be verified by the concerned department.

Step 7: Receive Booking Confirmation

Upon approval and payment confirmation, a booking confirmation or allotment letter will be issued.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Book the hall well in advance, especially during peak seasons.
  • Carry the booking confirmation on the event day.
  • Follow all hall usage rules and timing restrictions.
  • Ensure the premises are left clean after the event.
