🏠 House Tax Correction Application

A House Tax Correction Application is submitted to rectify errors in municipal property tax records such as owner name, property address, covered area, property category, contact details, or other assessment-related information.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Property Tax Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Application for House Tax Record Correction

Respected Sir/Madam,

I request you to kindly correct the details in the municipal house tax records for the property mentioned below:

  • Property ID / UPIC No.: ______________________
  • Name of Owner: ______________________
  • Property Address: ______________________
  • Current Incorrect Detail: ______________________
  • Correct Detail: ______________________
  • Reason for Correction: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________

I have attached the necessary supporting documents for verification. Kindly make the required corrections in the property tax records at the earliest.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for House Tax Correction

Step 1: Visit the Municipal Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation property tax portal and select the "House Tax Correction" service.

Step 2: Fill the Application Form

Enter the property details and specify the incorrect information along with the correct details.

Step 3: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof
  • Property Ownership Documents
  • Latest Property Tax Receipt
  • Address Proof
  • Supporting Documents for Correction

Step 4: Submit Application

Verify all details carefully and submit the correction request.

Step 5: Verification Process

Municipal authorities will review the submitted documents and verify the requested corrections.

Step 6: Record Update

Upon approval, the corrected information will be updated in the municipal property tax records.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Ensure all supporting documents are clear and valid.
  • Double-check the corrected information before submission.
  • Keep a copy of the acknowledgement receipt.
  • Track the application status regularly through the portal.
