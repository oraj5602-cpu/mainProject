💍 Marriage Registration Application

Marriage Registration is the official process of recording a marriage with the government. After successful registration, a Marriage Certificate is issued, which serves as legal proof of marriage for various purposes such as passport applications, visa processing, bank nominations, and other official matters.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Marriage Registrar,
Municipal Corporation / Sub-Divisional Magistrate Office,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Marriage Registration

📌 Guide: How to Apply for Marriage Registration

Step 1: Visit the Registration Portal

Visit the official Marriage Registration portal or the concerned SDM office website.

Step 2: Fill the Application Form

Enter details of both spouses including names, addresses, age, date of marriage, and place of marriage.

Step 3: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof of both spouses
  • Address Proof of both spouses
  • Age Proof documents
  • Marriage Photograph
  • Marriage Invitation Card (if available)
  • Passport-size Photographs
  • Witness Identity Proofs

Step 4: Schedule Appointment

Select a convenient date for document verification and appearance before the Marriage Registrar.

Step 5: Verification Process

Both spouses and witnesses must appear before the Registrar with original documents for verification.

Step 6: Certificate Issuance

After successful verification and approval, the Marriage Certificate will be issued and can be collected or downloaded online.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Ensure all documents are valid and up to date.
  • Carry original documents during verification.
  • Check that names and dates match across all documents.
  • Keep a copy of the application number for tracking purposes.
