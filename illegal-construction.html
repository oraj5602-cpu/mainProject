🏗️ Illegal Construction Complaint Application

An Illegal Construction Complaint Application is used to report unauthorized building activities, construction without approval, violations of sanctioned building plans, encroachment on public land, or any construction that appears to violate municipal regulations.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Building Department Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Complaint Regarding Illegal Construction

Respected Sir/Madam,

I would like to bring to your notice an alleged illegal construction activity taking place at the location mentioned below. The details are as follows:

  • Name of Complainant: ______________________
  • Address: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Location of Construction: ______________________
  • Nature of Complaint: Unauthorized Construction / Building Plan Violation / Encroachment / Other
  • Date Issue Noticed: ______________________

The construction activity appears to be in violation of applicable municipal rules and may affect public safety or nearby residents. I request the concerned department to investigate the matter and take appropriate action as per law.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Complainant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to File an Illegal Construction Complaint

Step 1: Visit the Municipal Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation grievance portal and select the "Illegal Construction Complaint" service.

Step 2: Fill the Complaint Form

Provide your contact information and details of the suspected illegal construction activity.

Step 3: Upload Supporting Evidence

  • Photographs of the Construction Site
  • Location Details
  • Any Relevant Documents or Evidence

Step 4: Submit the Complaint

Verify the information carefully and submit the complaint online.

Step 5: Receive Complaint Number

A complaint reference number will be generated for tracking purposes.

Step 6: Investigation & Action

The Building Department may inspect the site, verify records, and take action in accordance with municipal laws and regulations.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Provide the exact location of the construction site.
  • Upload clear photographs whenever possible.
  • Include specific details about the alleged violation.
  • Keep the complaint number safe for status tracking.
