📄 Death Certificate Application

A Death Certificate is an official government document issued by the Municipal Corporation that records the date, time, and cause of death of a person. It is required for legal formalities such as inheritance, insurance claims, property transfer, and other official procedures.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Registrar of Birth & Deaths,
Municipal Corporation Office,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Issuance of Death Certificate

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby request you to kindly issue the Death Certificate of the deceased person mentioned below:

  • Name of Deceased: ______________________
  • Date of Death: ______________________
  • Place of Death: ______________________
  • Father/Husband Name: ______________________
  • Age at the time of Death: ______________________
  • Address: ______________________

I request you to kindly process my application at the earliest. I shall be highly obliged.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Contact Number)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for Death Certificate (Online/Offline)

Step 1: Visit Municipal Portal

Go to your local Municipal Corporation website (MCD/EDMC/NDMC depending on your area).

Step 2: Select Birth & Death Services

Choose the “Death Certificate Registration” option under citizen services.

Step 3: Fill Application Form

Enter details like name of deceased, date of death, place of death, and applicant details.

Step 4: Upload Documents

  • Hospital Death Report (if available)
  • Identity proof of applicant
  • Proof of residence
  • Any supporting medical documents

Step 5: Submit Application

Review all details carefully and submit the form. Save your application number.

Step 6: Certificate Download

After verification, the Death Certificate can be downloaded from the portal or collected from the office.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Apply within the required time limit after death registration.
  • Ensure all details match hospital records.
  • Keep application number safe for tracking.
