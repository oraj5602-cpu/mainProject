🚽 Sewer Connection Application

A Sewer Connection Application is submitted to obtain a new sewerage connection for a residential, commercial, or institutional property. The connection enables the safe disposal of wastewater through the municipal sewer network.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Executive Engineer (Sewerage),
Municipal Corporation / Concerned Sewer Department,
Delhi

Subject: Application for New Sewer Connection

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby request you to provide a new sewer connection for the property mentioned below. The necessary details are furnished herewith:

  • Applicant Name: ______________________
  • Father's/Husband's Name: ______________________
  • Property Address: ______________________
  • Property Type: Residential / Commercial / Institutional
  • Property ID / House No.: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________

I have enclosed the required documents in support of this application. Kindly process my request and grant the sewer connection at the earliest.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for a Sewer Connection

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the municipal or sewer department portal and select the "New Sewer Connection" service.

Step 2: Fill Out the Application Form

Enter all required applicant and property details accurately.

Step 3: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Address Proof
  • Property Ownership Documents
  • Latest Property Tax Receipt
  • Building Completion/Occupancy Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size Photograph

Step 4: Pay Applicable Fees

Pay the prescribed sewer connection charges through the available payment methods.

Step 5: Technical Inspection

The concerned department may conduct a site inspection to verify the property's suitability for connection to the sewer network.

Step 6: Approval & Connection Installation

Upon successful verification and approval, the sewer connection will be installed and activated.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Ensure all submitted documents are valid and legible.
  • Verify property details before submitting the application.
  • Keep copies of payment receipts and acknowledgement numbers.
  • Track the application status regularly through the portal.
