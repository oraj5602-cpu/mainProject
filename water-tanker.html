🚛 Water Tanker Booking Application

A Water Tanker Booking Application is used to request the supply of water through a tanker for residential, commercial, institutional, or emergency purposes when regular water supply is unavailable or insufficient.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Water Supply Officer,
Municipal Corporation / Water Department,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Water Tanker Supply

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby request the supply of a water tanker at the address mentioned below. The details of the request are as follows:

  • Name of Applicant: ______________________
  • Residential/Commercial Address: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________
  • Purpose of Water Requirement: ______________________
  • Required Date: ______________________
  • Preferred Time: ______________________
  • Quantity Required (if applicable): ______________________

I request you to kindly arrange the water tanker supply at the earliest. I shall comply with all applicable terms and conditions.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Book a Water Tanker

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation or Water Department portal and select the "Water Tanker Booking" service.

Step 2: Register/Login

Create an account or log in using your existing credentials.

Step 3: Fill the Booking Form

Enter your address, contact details, water requirement, and preferred delivery date and time.

Step 4: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Water Connection Details (if applicable)
  • Any Additional Documents Required by the Authority

Step 5: Pay Applicable Charges

Complete the payment of tanker booking charges, if applicable.

Step 6: Booking Confirmation

After successful submission, a booking reference number will be generated.

Step 7: Water Tanker Delivery

The concerned department will schedule and dispatch the tanker to the requested location as per availability.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Provide a complete and accurate delivery address.
  • Book in advance during periods of high demand.
  • Keep the booking reference number safe.
  • Ensure easy access for the tanker at the delivery location.
