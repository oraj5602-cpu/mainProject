🗑️ Garbage Collection Complaint Application

A Garbage Collection Complaint Application is used to report issues such as non-collection of garbage, overflowing waste bins, irregular waste collection, unattended garbage dumps, or unsanitary waste disposal in municipal areas.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Sanitation Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Complaint Regarding Garbage Collection Issue

Respected Sir/Madam,

I would like to report a garbage collection issue in my locality. The details of the complaint are provided below:

  • Name of Complainant: ______________________
  • Address: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Location of Issue: ______________________
  • Nature of Complaint: Garbage Not Collected / Overflowing Bin / Open Dumping / Irregular Collection
  • Date Issue Noticed: ______________________

The accumulation of garbage is causing inconvenience and may create health and environmental hazards. I request the concerned department to take immediate action to resolve the issue.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Complainant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to File a Garbage Collection Complaint

Step 1: Visit the Municipal Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation grievance portal and select the "Garbage Collection Complaint" service.

Step 2: Fill the Complaint Form

Enter your contact details and provide complete information about the garbage collection issue.

Step 3: Upload Supporting Evidence

  • Photographs of the Garbage Issue
  • Exact Location Details
  • Any Additional Information

Step 4: Submit the Complaint

Review all details carefully and submit the complaint online.

Step 5: Receive Complaint Number

A complaint reference number will be generated for tracking purposes.

Step 6: Inspection & Resolution

The sanitation department may inspect the reported location and arrange for garbage removal or corrective action.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Provide accurate location information.
  • Upload clear photographs to support the complaint.
  • Report the issue as soon as it is noticed.
  • Keep the complaint number safe for future reference.
