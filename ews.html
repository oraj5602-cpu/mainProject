📋 Guide to Apply for an EWS Certificate

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Ensure that you belong to the General Category and meet the income and asset criteria prescribed by the Government for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS).

Step 2: Collect Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Identity Proof (Voter ID/PAN Card/Driving Licence, etc.)
  • Address Proof
  • Income Certificate or Income Proof
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Self Declaration (if required)
  • Any other document requested by the local authority

Step 3: Fill the Application Form

Fill the EWS Certificate Application Form carefully with correct personal details, address, family income information, and purpose of the certificate.

Step 4: Submit the Application

Submit the completed form along with all supporting documents to the Tehsil Office, SDM Office, CSC Centre, or through the State Government's online portal (where available).

Step 5: Verification Process

The concerned authority will verify your documents and may conduct additional verification if necessary.

Step 6: Certificate Issuance

After successful verification, the EWS Certificate will be issued. You can collect it from the office or download it online if your state provides digital services.

Important Notes

  • Provide only genuine documents and information.
  • Incomplete applications may be rejected.
  • Keep photocopies of all submitted documents for future reference.
  • Processing time varies by state and district.
