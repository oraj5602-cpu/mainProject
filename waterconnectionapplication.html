🚰 Water Connection Application

A Water Connection Application is submitted to obtain a new water supply connection for a residential, commercial, or institutional property. The connection is provided after verification of documents and approval by the concerned authority.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Executive Engineer (Water Supply),
Municipal Corporation / Concerned Water Department,
Delhi

Subject: Application for New Water Connection

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby request you to provide a new water connection for the property mentioned below. The required details are furnished herewith:

  • Applicant Name: ______________________
  • Father's/Husband's Name: ______________________
  • Property Address: ______________________
  • Property Type: Residential / Commercial / Institutional
  • Property ID / House No.: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________

I have attached all necessary documents in support of my application. Kindly process the request and grant a water connection at the earliest.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for a Water Connection

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the official water supply or municipal services portal and navigate to the "New Water Connection" section.

Step 2: Complete the Application Form

Enter applicant details, property information, and connection requirements.

Step 3: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Address Proof
  • Property Ownership Documents
  • Recent Property Tax Receipt
  • Building Completion/Occupancy Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size Photograph

Step 4: Pay Applicable Charges

Pay the prescribed connection fee and any additional charges through the available online or offline payment methods.

Step 5: Site Inspection

Officials may inspect the property to verify the details and assess the feasibility of providing the connection.

Step 6: Connection Approval & Installation

After successful verification and approval, the water connection and meter (if applicable) will be installed.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Ensure all property details are entered correctly.
  • Upload clear and valid supporting documents.
  • Keep payment receipts and acknowledgement numbers safe.
  • Regularly check the application status through the portal.
