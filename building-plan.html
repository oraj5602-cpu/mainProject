🏗️ Building Plan Approval Application

Building Plan Approval is required before starting the construction, reconstruction, alteration, or extension of a building. The approval ensures that the proposed construction complies with municipal building bye-laws, zoning regulations, and safety standards.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Building Department Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Approval of Building Plan

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby submit my application for approval of the building plan for the property mentioned below. The required details are as follows:

  • Name of Applicant: ______________________
  • Father's/Husband's Name: ______________________
  • Property Address: ______________________
  • Property ID / Plot No.: ______________________
  • Land Area: ______________________
  • Proposed Construction Type: Residential / Commercial / Institutional
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________

I have enclosed the building drawings, ownership documents, and other required records for verification. Kindly approve the submitted building plan and grant permission for construction.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for Building Plan Approval

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation Building Plan Approval System and select the building permission service.

Step 2: Register/Login

Create an account or sign in to your existing account.

Step 3: Fill the Application Form

Enter property details, ownership information, and construction details accurately.

Step 4: Upload Required Documents

  • Ownership Documents / Sale Deed
  • Site Plan
  • Architectural Drawings
  • Structural Safety Certificate
  • Identity Proof of Applicant
  • Latest Property Tax Receipt
  • Any Required NOCs

Step 5: Pay Application Fee

Pay the prescribed scrutiny and approval fees through the available payment options.

Step 6: Technical Scrutiny

Municipal engineers and planning officials will review the submitted plans for compliance with applicable regulations.

Step 7: Approval & Permission

Upon successful verification and compliance, the Building Plan Approval Certificate will be issued, allowing construction work to commence.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Do not begin construction before receiving approval.
  • Ensure plans are prepared by a licensed architect or engineer.
  • Upload clear and complete documents.
  • Keep the application number for future tracking and reference.
