🏢 Trade License Application

A Trade License is an official permission issued by the Municipal Corporation allowing an individual or business to carry out a specific trade or commercial activity within municipal limits. It ensures that the business complies with applicable laws, safety standards, and public health regulations.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Licensing Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Grant of Trade License

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby apply for the issuance of a Trade License for operating the business mentioned below. The required particulars are as follows:

  • Name of Applicant: ______________________
  • Business Name: ______________________
  • Nature of Trade/Business: ______________________
  • Business Address: ______________________
  • Type of Premises: Owned / Rented
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________

I have attached all necessary documents required for obtaining the license. Kindly verify my application and grant the Trade License at the earliest.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for a Trade License

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation's online portal and navigate to the "Trade License" section.

Step 2: Register/Login

Create an account or log in using your existing credentials.

Step 3: Fill the Application Form

Provide details regarding the business, applicant, premises, and nature of the trade.

Step 4: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof of Applicant
  • Address Proof
  • Property Ownership/Rent Agreement
  • No Objection Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Business Registration Documents (if applicable)

Step 5: Pay License Fee

Pay the prescribed trade license fee through the available online or offline payment options.

Step 6: Verification & Inspection

Municipal officials may inspect the business premises to verify the information provided in the application.

Step 7: License Issuance

Upon successful verification and approval, the Trade License will be issued and can be downloaded or collected from the concerned office.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Ensure the business activity complies with municipal regulations.
  • Upload clear and valid supporting documents.
  • Keep the application number and payment receipt safe.
  • Renew the Trade License before its expiry date.
