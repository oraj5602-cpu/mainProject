🧹 Sanitation Complaint Application

A Sanitation Complaint Application is used to report issues related to cleanliness, unhygienic conditions, accumulated waste, public health hazards, overflowing bins, or improper sanitation services within municipal areas.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Sanitation Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Complaint Regarding Sanitation Issue

Respected Sir/Madam,

I would like to bring to your notice a sanitation-related issue in my locality. The details of the complaint are provided below:

  • Name of Complainant: ______________________
  • Address: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Location of Issue: ______________________
  • Nature of Complaint: ______________________
  • Date Issue Noticed: ______________________

The issue is causing inconvenience and may pose health risks to residents. I request the concerned department to inspect the matter and take necessary corrective action at the earliest.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Complainant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to File a Sanitation Complaint

Step 1: Visit the Municipal Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation grievance portal and select the "Sanitation Complaint" service.

Step 2: Fill the Complaint Form

Enter your contact information and provide complete details about the sanitation issue.

Step 3: Upload Supporting Evidence

  • Photographs of the Issue
  • Location Details
  • Any Additional Supporting Information

Step 4: Submit the Complaint

Review the information carefully and submit the complaint online.

Step 5: Receive Complaint Number

A complaint reference number will be generated for future tracking.

Step 6: Inspection & Resolution

The sanitation department may inspect the reported location and take appropriate action to resolve the issue.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Provide accurate location information.
  • Attach clear photographs for faster processing.
  • Describe the issue in detail.
  • Keep the complaint number safe for status tracking.
