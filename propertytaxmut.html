🏠 Property Tax Mutation Application

Property Tax Mutation is the process of updating the ownership details of a property in municipal records after a sale, inheritance, gift deed, partition, or other lawful transfer. Mutation helps ensure that future property tax records reflect the correct owner's name.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Property Tax Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Property Tax Mutation

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby request you to mutate the property mentioned below in my name and update the municipal property tax records accordingly.

  • Name of Previous Owner: ______________________
  • Name of New Owner: ______________________
  • Property Address: ______________________
  • Property ID / UPIC No.: ______________________
  • Transfer Type: Sale / Gift / Inheritance / Partition / Other
  • Date of Transfer: ______________________
  • Contact Number: ______________________

I have enclosed the necessary documents in support of this application. Kindly verify the records and update the ownership details at the earliest.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for Property Tax Mutation

Step 1: Visit the Municipal Portal

Open the official Municipal Corporation property tax portal and select the "Property Tax Mutation" service.

Step 2: Fill the Application Form

Enter the property details, previous owner information, new owner information, and the basis of transfer.

Step 3: Upload Required Documents

  • Registered Sale Deed / Gift Deed / Will
  • Property Tax Receipt
  • Identity Proof of Applicant
  • Address Proof of Applicant
  • No Objection Certificate (if applicable)
  • Death Certificate (for inheritance cases)
  • Any other supporting documents

Step 4: Submit Application

Verify all entered details and submit the application. Save the acknowledgement or application number for future reference.

Step 5: Verification by Municipal Authority

The concerned department may verify the submitted documents and property records before approving the mutation request.

Step 6: Mutation Approval

Once approved, the property tax records will be updated with the new owner's name, and future tax bills will be generated accordingly.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Ensure that all property taxes are cleared before applying.
  • Upload clear and legible copies of documents.
  • Double-check the property ID and owner details.
  • Keep the acknowledgement number safe for status tracking.
