💡 Street Light Complaint Application

A Street Light Complaint Application is used to report non-functional, damaged, flickering, broken, or missing street lights in public areas. Proper street lighting helps ensure road safety, public security, and convenience for residents.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Electrical Maintenance Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Complaint Regarding Defective Street Light

Respected Sir/Madam,

I would like to report a street light issue in my locality. The details of the complaint are as follows:

  • Name of Complainant: ______________________
  • Address: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Location of Street Light: ______________________
  • Pole Number (if known): ______________________
  • Nature of Complaint: Not Working / Flickering / Damaged / Missing
  • Date Issue Noticed: ______________________

The issue is causing inconvenience and may affect public safety during nighttime. I kindly request the concerned department to inspect and rectify the problem at the earliest.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Complainant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to File a Street Light Complaint

Step 1: Visit the Municipal Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation grievance portal and select the "Street Light Complaint" service.

Step 2: Fill the Complaint Form

Enter your contact details and provide the exact location of the defective street light.

Step 3: Upload Supporting Evidence

  • Photograph of the Street Light (if available)
  • Location Details
  • Pole Number (if visible)

Step 4: Submit the Complaint

Verify the information and submit the complaint online.

Step 5: Receive Complaint Number

A complaint reference number will be generated for tracking purposes.

Step 6: Inspection & Repair

The electrical maintenance team may inspect the reported location and carry out the necessary repair or replacement work.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Provide the exact location of the street light.
  • Include the pole number if available.
  • Upload clear photographs whenever possible.
  • Keep the complaint number safe for status tracking.
