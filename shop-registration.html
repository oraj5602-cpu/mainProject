🏪 Shop Registration Application

A Shop Registration Application is submitted to register a shop or commercial establishment under the applicable laws and municipal regulations. Registration helps establish the legal identity of the business and may be required for various licenses, permits, and banking purposes.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Registration Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Shop Registration

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby apply for the registration of my shop/commercial establishment. The details of the business are as follows:

  • Name of Applicant: ______________________
  • Name of Shop/Establishment: ______________________
  • Nature of Business: ______________________
  • Business Address: ______________________
  • Date of Commencement: ______________________
  • Number of Employees: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________

I have enclosed all the required documents in support of this application. Kindly process my request and grant registration for the above-mentioned establishment.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for Shop Registration

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation or Labour Department portal and select the "Shop Registration" service.

Step 2: Register/Login

Create a new account or sign in using your existing credentials.

Step 3: Fill the Application Form

Enter details of the applicant, business, employees, and establishment.

Step 4: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof of Applicant
  • Address Proof
  • Proof of Business Address
  • Rent Agreement / Ownership Documents
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Business Registration Documents (if applicable)

Step 5: Pay Registration Fee

Pay the prescribed registration fee through the available payment methods.

Step 6: Verification

The concerned authority may verify the submitted details and documents.

Step 7: Registration Certificate Issuance

Upon approval, the Shop Registration Certificate will be issued and can be downloaded or collected from the concerned office.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Ensure all business details are entered correctly.
  • Upload clear and valid supporting documents.
  • Keep payment receipts and acknowledgement numbers safe.
  • Renew or update registration details whenever required.
