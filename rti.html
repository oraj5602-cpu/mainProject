How to File an RTI Application

  1. Identify the Government Department that holds the information you need.
  2. Write your application clearly mentioning the information required.
  3. Address the application to the Public Information Officer (PIO) of that department.
  4. Include your:
    • Name
    • Address
    • Contact Number
    • Email (Optional)
  5. Pay the prescribed RTI application fee (if applicable).
  6. Submit the application online or at the concerned office.
  7. Keep the acknowledgement or receipt safely.
  8. The Public Information Officer generally replies within 30 days under the RTI Act, unless a different statutory timeline applies.

Tip: Ask specific questions and avoid vague requests. Clear applications usually receive faster and more useful responses.
