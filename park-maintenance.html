🌳 Park Maintenance Complaint Application

A Park Maintenance Complaint Application is used to report issues related to public parks such as damaged equipment, broken benches, poor cleanliness, overgrown grass, non-functional lights, waterlogging, or lack of maintenance.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Horticulture Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Complaint Regarding Park Maintenance

Respected Sir/Madam,

I would like to bring to your notice certain maintenance issues in a public park located in my locality. The details are as follows:

  • Name of Complainant: ______________________
  • Address: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Name/Location of Park: ______________________
  • Nature of Complaint: ______________________
  • Date Issue Noticed: ______________________

The issue is affecting the proper use and maintenance of the park. I kindly request the concerned department to inspect the site and take the necessary corrective measures.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Complainant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to File a Park Maintenance Complaint

Step 1: Visit the Municipal Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation grievance portal and select the "Park Maintenance Complaint" service.

Step 2: Fill the Complaint Form

Enter your contact details and provide complete information about the park and the maintenance issue.

Step 3: Upload Supporting Evidence

  • Photographs of the Issue
  • Location of the Park
  • Additional Remarks (if any)

Step 4: Submit the Complaint

Review the information carefully and submit the complaint.

Step 5: Receive Complaint Number

A complaint reference number will be generated for tracking the status of your complaint.

Step 6: Inspection & Maintenance Work

The horticulture or park maintenance department may inspect the site and carry out the required maintenance work.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Provide the exact location of the park.
  • Describe the issue clearly for faster resolution.
  • Upload photographs whenever possible.
  • Keep the complaint number safe for future reference.
