📢 Advertisement Hoarding Permission Application

An Advertisement Hoarding Permission Application is submitted to obtain approval for installing advertisement boards, banners, hoardings, display signs, digital screens, or other advertising structures within municipal limits. Permission is required to ensure compliance with safety standards and municipal advertising regulations.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Advertisement Department Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Advertisement Hoarding Permission

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby request permission to install an advertisement display at the location mentioned below. The details are as follows:

  • Name of Applicant/Company: ______________________
  • Business Name: ______________________
  • Address: ______________________
  • Type of Advertisement: Hoarding / Banner / Signboard / Digital Display
  • Location of Installation: ______________________
  • Dimensions of Advertisement: ______________________
  • Display Duration: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________

I undertake to comply with all municipal rules and safety requirements regarding advertisement displays. Kindly approve my application and grant the necessary permission.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for Advertisement Hoarding Permission

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation portal and navigate to the "Advertisement Permission" section.

Step 2: Register/Login

Create an account or log in with your existing credentials.

Step 3: Fill the Application Form

Enter applicant details, advertisement specifications, location, and display duration.

Step 4: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof of Applicant
  • Business Registration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Site Plan / Location Map
  • Design/Layout of Advertisement
  • Property Owner's NOC (if required)
  • Address Proof

Step 5: Pay Prescribed Fee

Submit the applicable advertisement permission fee through the available payment methods.

Step 6: Site Inspection & Verification

Municipal officials may inspect the proposed site to ensure compliance with regulations and safety requirements.

Step 7: Permission Approval

Upon approval, the advertisement permission certificate will be issued, allowing installation of the approved display.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Do not install any advertisement without prior approval.
  • Ensure the advertisement does not obstruct traffic or public spaces.
  • Follow all size and safety guidelines prescribed by the authority.
  • Keep the approval certificate available for inspection.
