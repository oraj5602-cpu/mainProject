Domicile Certificate Application Guide

  1. Visit your State Government's online citizen service portal or your nearest Tehsil/CSC office.
  2. Log in or create a new account.
  3. Select "Domicile Certificate" from the list of available services.
  4. Fill in the application form with:
    • Applicant Name
    • Father's/Mother's Name
    • Date of Birth
    • Permanent Address
    • State/District
    • Mobile Number
  5. Upload the required documents such as:
    • Aadhaar Card
    • Residence Proof
    • Identity Proof
    • Birth Certificate or School Certificate (if required)
    • Passport-size Photograph (if applicable)
  6. Verify all details carefully.
  7. Pay the application fee if applicable.
  8. Submit the application.
  9. Save the Application Number or Acknowledgement Receipt.
  10. Track the application status and download the certificate after approval.

Note: The required documents and procedure may vary depending on your State or Union Territory. Always provide correct information to avoid delays.
