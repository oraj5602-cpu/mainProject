🐕 Stray Animal Complaint Application

A Stray Animal Complaint Application is used to report issues related to stray dogs, cattle, monkeys, pigs, or other animals causing inconvenience, safety concerns, traffic obstruction, or public health issues within municipal areas.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Veterinary Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Complaint Regarding Stray Animal Issue

Respected Sir/Madam,

I would like to report the presence of stray animals in my locality. The details of the complaint are provided below:

  • Name of Complainant: ______________________
  • Address: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Location of Issue: ______________________
  • Type of Animal: Dog / Cattle / Monkey / Pig / Other
  • Number of Animals (Approx.): ______________________
  • Nature of Complaint: ______________________
  • Date Issue Noticed: ______________________

The presence of these animals is causing inconvenience and may pose a risk to public safety and hygiene. I request the concerned department to inspect the matter and take necessary action.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Complainant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to File a Stray Animal Complaint

Step 1: Visit the Municipal Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation grievance portal and select the "Stray Animal Complaint" service.

Step 2: Fill the Complaint Form

Enter your contact details and provide information about the animal-related issue.

Step 3: Upload Supporting Evidence

  • Photographs of the Animal(s)
  • Exact Location Details
  • Additional Remarks (if any)

Step 4: Submit the Complaint

Verify all information and submit the complaint online.

Step 5: Receive Complaint Number

A complaint reference number will be generated for status tracking.

Step 6: Inspection & Necessary Action

The concerned veterinary or animal control department may inspect the location and take appropriate action as per municipal guidelines.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Provide the exact location where the animals are frequently seen.
  • Upload clear photographs if possible.
  • Do not attempt to capture or harm the animals yourself.
  • Keep the complaint number safe for future tracking.
