🏥 Health Trade License Application

A Health Trade License is required for businesses involved in food handling, healthcare services, beauty and wellness services, lodging, and other activities that may affect public health and hygiene. The license ensures compliance with municipal health and sanitation standards.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Health Licensing Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Application for Grant of Health Trade License

Respected Sir/Madam,

I hereby apply for the issuance of a Health Trade License for the establishment mentioned below. The required particulars are furnished herewith:

  • Name of Applicant: ______________________
  • Name of Establishment: ______________________
  • Nature of Business: ______________________
  • Business Address: ______________________
  • Type of Premises: Owned / Rented
  • Number of Employees: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Email ID: ______________________

I have enclosed the necessary documents and undertake to comply with all applicable health, sanitation, and safety regulations. Kindly process my application and grant the Health Trade License.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Applicant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to Apply for a Health Trade License

Step 1: Visit the Official Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation portal and select the "Health Trade License" service from the licensing section.

Step 2: Register/Login

Create a new account or log in using your existing credentials.

Step 3: Fill the Application Form

Enter the details of the applicant, establishment, business activity, and premises.

Step 4: Upload Required Documents

  • Identity Proof of Applicant
  • Address Proof
  • Property Ownership Proof / Rent Agreement
  • Site Plan of Premises
  • No Objection Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Food Safety License (where applicable)

Step 5: Pay Prescribed Fee

Submit the required application fee through the available payment methods.

Step 6: Health Inspection

Municipal health officials may inspect the premises to verify sanitation, hygiene, waste disposal, and public health compliance.

Step 7: License Approval & Issuance

Upon successful verification and approval, the Health Trade License will be issued and made available for download or collection.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Maintain proper hygiene and sanitation standards at all times.
  • Keep all health-related certifications updated.
  • Ensure accurate information is provided in the application.
  • Renew the Health Trade License before its expiry date.
