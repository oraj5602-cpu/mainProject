🚰 Drain Cleaning Complaint Application

A Drain Cleaning Complaint Application is used to report blocked drains, overflowing sewage, water stagnation, foul smell, clogged storm-water drains, or other drainage-related issues that may cause inconvenience, unhygienic conditions, or health hazards.

📝 Application Format

To,
The Sanitation Officer,
Municipal Corporation,
Delhi

Subject: Complaint Regarding Drain Cleaning

Respected Sir/Madam,

I would like to bring to your notice a drainage-related issue in my locality. The details of the complaint are as follows:

  • Name of Complainant: ______________________
  • Address: ______________________
  • Mobile Number: ______________________
  • Location of Drain: ______________________
  • Nature of Complaint: Blocked Drain / Overflowing Drain / Water Stagnation / Foul Smell / Other
  • Date Issue Noticed: ______________________

The drainage issue is causing inconvenience to residents and may create health and sanitation problems. I request the concerned department to inspect the matter and carry out the necessary cleaning and maintenance work.

Thanking You,

Yours faithfully,
______________________ (Complainant Name)
______________________ (Signature)
______________________ (Date)

📌 Guide: How to File a Drain Cleaning Complaint

Step 1: Visit the Municipal Portal

Open the Municipal Corporation grievance portal and select the "Drain Cleaning Complaint" service.

Step 2: Fill the Complaint Form

Enter your contact details and provide complete information about the drainage issue.

Step 3: Upload Supporting Evidence

  • Photographs of the Drain Issue
  • Exact Location Details
  • Additional Remarks (if any)

Step 4: Submit the Complaint

Review the details carefully and submit the complaint online.

Step 5: Receive Complaint Number

A complaint reference number will be generated for tracking the status of the complaint.

Step 6: Inspection & Cleaning

The concerned sanitation department may inspect the location and carry out drain cleaning, desilting, or maintenance work as required.

⚠️ Important Tips

  • Provide the exact drain location or nearby landmark.
  • Upload clear photographs whenever possible.
  • Report severe overflow issues immediately.
  • Keep the complaint number safe for future tracking.
